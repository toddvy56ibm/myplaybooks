IBM Global Technology Services

Powering enterprise innovation with agile, resilient, cost-effective storage services in the cloud

IBM helps diverse clients expand to the cloud through storage as a service based on IBM Spectrum Accelerate and IBM Cloud.

Watch the video

>Learn more about IBM Services

“ We sell services--IBM Spectrum Accelerate is an 'enterprise storage box in the cloud' that lets us deliver SaaS at lower cost and without compromise. ”

— Jim Olson, Distinguished Engineer, IBM

Business Challenge story

One of the world's largest service providers found clients needed an agile platform for innovation, for business continuity, and to cut costs--all in harmony with their traditional environments.

Transformation

To help clients shift to cloud, IBM® Global Technology Services® deployed enterprise storage-as-a-service using IBM Spectrum Accelerate™ software and IBM Cloud.

Benefits

The solution delivers performance, resiliency and agility while modernizing through support of new workloads and transforming with use-cases like DevOps--all at attractive costs.
